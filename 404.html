A Biriyani Story

A feast of aromatic and flavourful Biryani is a dream come true for food lovers.Our simple style of biryani is sure to delight everyone's heart and appetite. The amalgamation of spices ground to perfection , tender succulent meat, farm fresh vegetables and aged Kaima rice, cooked in traditional DUM style, ensures consistent quality and taste in every bite. Choose from our choice of chicken or beef biryani, which is sure to leave our customers craving for more.

Our mission is to serve Biryani to everyone no matter the size. The delicacy of our biryani is now available for kids too.
